AWS Certification Roadmap

Source: aws.amazon.com/certification  ·  Last updated: May 2026  ·  1.42M+ active certifications  ·  1.05M+ unique certified individuals

AWS certifications validate cloud skills across four progressively advanced levels — from non-technical business professionals to senior ML engineers. They're valid for 3 years and delivered via Pearson VUE (test center or online proctored).

Tip: Passing Cloud Practitioner earns a 50% discount voucher for your next AWS exam. Start there if you're new to AWS.

Foundational Level

Goal: Build cloud literacy and core AWS knowledge. No prior experience required.

AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner CLF-C02

Foundational $100 90 min 65 questions Valid 3 years

The recommended starting point for anyone new to AWS. Earns a 50% discount voucher for the next exam.

Who it's for: Individuals new to cloud; non-technical roles (sales, marketing, finance, project management); business leaders needing cloud literacy.

What it validates: High-level AWS service knowledge, cloud value proposition, shared responsibility model, pricing, security fundamentals.

Recertification options: Pass the updated exam, complete AWS Cloud Quest game-based learning (free through July 2025), or earn any Associate/Professional cert.

Salary at this stage: $60K–$90K/yr (entry cloud & IT roles). Demand: 9,000+ job postings on LinkedIn — 70% entry-level.

→ Full CLF-C02 Exam Guide

AWS Certified AI Practitioner AIF-C01

Foundational $100 90 min 65 questions Valid 3 years

The foundational entry point for AI/ML on AWS. Focused on concepts and services, not hands-on engineering.

Who it's for: Business analysts, IT support, marketing/sales professionals, product managers — anyone familiar with AI tools but not building systems.

What it validates: Foundational AI/ML/GenAI concepts, AWS AI service landscape, responsible AI principles.

Recommended path before this cert: No prior AI experience required. New IT professionals: complete AWS Cloud Practitioner Essentials first. CLF holders can proceed directly.

Recertification: Pass current exam version or earn the ML Engineer Associate.

Salary at this stage: $80K–$120K/yr (AI-adjacent / support roles).

→ Full AIF-C01 Exam Guide

Associate Level

Goal: Validate core technical skills for specific AWS roles. Recommended: 1–2 years hands-on experience.

AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate SAA-C03

Associate $150 130 min 65 questions

The most popular AWS certification worldwide. Ranked in the top 10 certifications globally by Skillsoft.

Who it's for: Cloud architects, infrastructure engineers, IT professionals designing AWS solutions.

What it validates: Designing resilient, high-performing, and secure architectures; selecting appropriate AWS services; cost and performance optimisation.

Experience recommended: 1 year designing cloud solutions. CLF recommended for those without an IT background.

Salary: avg $153K/yr (range $100K–$234K). Demand: 2,000+ job postings.

→ Full SAA-C03 Exam Guide

AWS Certified Developer – Associate DVA-C02

Associate $150 130 min 65 questions

Who it's for: Software developers and application engineers building/deploying on AWS.

What it validates: Developing, testing, deploying, and debugging cloud-based apps; CI/CD workflows; Lambda, DynamoDB, deployment pipelines.

Recertification: Retake the exam or earn the DevOps Engineer Professional.

Salary: $120K–$145K/yr for cloud developer roles. Often combined with SAA for a stronger profile.

→ Full DVA-C02 Exam Guide

AWS Certified CloudOps Engineer – Associate

Associate New $150 130 min 65 questions

The evolution of SysOps Administrator — reflects modern cloud operations practices.

Who it's for: Cloud Operations Specialists, Cloud Support Engineers, Migration Specialists.

What it validates: Deploying and managing workloads on AWS; monitoring; security controls; networking; business continuity; cost optimisation.

Salary: $120K–$140K/yr for cloud operations roles.

AWS Certified SysOps Administrator – Associate SOA-C02

Associate Being superseded by CloudOps $150 130 min

Who it's for: System administrators and operations engineers managing AWS workloads.

What it validates: Deploying/managing AWS infrastructure; monitoring; security controls; business continuity; cost optimisation.

This exam is being superseded by the CloudOps Engineer Associate. New candidates should prefer the CloudOps path.

AWS Certified Data Engineer – Associate DEA-C01

Associate $150 130 min 65 questions

Who it's for: Data engineers and data architects building/managing data pipelines on AWS.

What it validates: Data ingestion, transformation, pipeline orchestration, data model design; AWS data services (Glue, Kinesis, Redshift, S3, Lake Formation).

Experience recommended: 2–3 years data engineering + 1–2 years hands-on AWS.

Salary: avg $137K/yr (range $89K–$211K). Demand: 2,000+ postings/month.

AWS Certified Machine Learning Engineer – Associate MLA-C01

Associate Replaces ML Specialty $150 130 min 65 questions

The new hands-on ML engineering cert for production workloads. Successor to the retiring ML Specialty.

Who it's for: Backend developers moving into ML; DevOps/data engineers; MLOps engineers; data scientists operationalising models.

What it validates: Implementing ML workloads in production on AWS; SageMaker; model deployment, monitoring, and ML pipeline operations.

Salary: avg $187K/yr (range $114K–$310K). Demand: 33,000+ postings — highest of any AWS path.

Professional Level

Goal: Demonstrate advanced AWS expertise for complex, enterprise-scale solutions. Requires 2+ years hands-on experience.

AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Professional SAP-C02

Professional $300 180 min 75 questions

Who it's for: Senior cloud architects designing complex multi-account AWS environments.

What it validates: Sophisticated solutions for complex cloud problems; security/cost/performance optimisation at scale; compliance, migration, and hybrid architectures; architectural guidance across large organisations.

Salary: avg $155K–$166K/yr (Senior Cloud Architect avg $165K). Demand: 2,000+ postings, majority senior/director level. Qualifies for government and finance sector roles.

AWS Certified DevOps Engineer – Professional DOP-C02

Professional $300 180 min 75 questions

Who it's for: DevOps engineers and platform engineers managing CI/CD and distributed systems.

What it validates: Provisioning/operating distributed apps on AWS; advanced CI/CD and infrastructure-as-code; monitoring and observability at scale; security and compliance automation.

Experience recommended: 2+ years AWS operations; scripting/programming familiarity.

Salary: DevOps Engineer avg $132K/yr; Senior DevOps avg $151K/yr (range $85K–$205K). Demand: 1,000+ postings (495 new/week).

AWS Certified Generative AI Developer – Professional

Professional New 2025 $300 180 min 75 questions

The most advanced AI engineering credential on AWS. Brand new in 2025.

Who it's for: AI/ML engineers building production GenAI systems; architects designing agentic AI on AWS.

What it validates: Production-ready GenAI on AWS; Amazon Bedrock and foundation models; agentic AI workflows and RAG systems; responsible AI in production; cost optimisation for AI workloads.

Recommended prerequisites (not mandatory): AI Practitioner, Solutions Architect Associate, ML Engineer Associate, Data Engineer Associate.

Salary: AI Developer avg $153K/yr; top-tier roles up to $500K. Top cities: San Jose $204K, San Francisco $195K, New York $182K. Demand: 11,000+ postings, growing rapidly.

Specialty Level

Goal: Demonstrate deep, domain-specific expertise. Requires significant real-world experience.

AWS Certified Security – Specialty SCS-C02

Specialty $300 170 min 65 questions

One of the highest-paying and fastest-growing AWS certifications.

Who it's for: Security engineers, architects, and analysts responsible for AWS security posture.

What it validates: Creating and implementing security solutions; data classification and protection; encryption; IAM at scale; incident response; threat detection; secure networking.

Experience recommended: 5+ years IT security + 2+ years hands-on AWS security.

Market signal: Job listings grew 73% YoY. Consistently ranked among the highest-paying technical certifications globally.

Salary: avg $132K/yr (range $87K–$202K). Unlocks healthcare, finance, government, and defense roles (FedRAMP, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, SOC 2). Demand: 429+ targeted postings.

AWS Certified Advanced Networking – Specialty ANS-C01

Specialty $300 170 min 65 questions

Who it's for: Senior network engineers designing complex hybrid and cloud-native topologies.

What it validates: Complex networking tasks; multi-region solutions; advanced protocols (IP VPN, MPLS, BGP); network automation; AWS WAF, IDS/IPS, DDoS protection.

Experience recommended: 5+ years networking + 2+ years cloud/hybrid. One of the most experience-intensive certs in the catalog.

Salary: Senior Network Engineer avg $127K/yr (range $70K–$188K). Demand: 1,000+ postings. Low supply of certified candidates keeps salaries competitive.

AWS Certified Machine Learning – Specialty MLS-C01

Specialty ⚠ Retiring March 31, 2026 $300 180 min
Retiring March 31, 2026. New candidates should pursue the ML Engineer – Associate (MLA-C01) instead.

Who it's for: Data scientists and ML engineers with deep AWS expertise.

What it validates: Designing, building, deploying, and optimising ML solutions on AWS; deep learning model development; selecting/tuning AWS ML services.

Important Caveats

  • Study hours only — add 1–2 weeks buffer per cert for scheduling and review time.
  • Knowledge compounds — passing a lower cert reduces hours for the next. Associate study covers ~30% of Professional content.
  • Practice exams are included in the totals. AWS recommends at least one full official practice exam per cert.
  • Hands-on lab time counts. AWS Builder Labs or real projects count toward study hours — and make you more efficient.

Exam Tips & Resources

AWS Recommended 4-Step Prep Plan

  1. Review the Exam Guide — download the official guide, study the domains and percentage weights
  2. Take Official Practice Questions — identify knowledge gaps with pretests on AWS Skill Builder
  3. Complete Digital Courses & Labs — fill gaps via Skill Builder courses, Builder Labs, and Cloud Quest
  4. Take the Official Practice Exam — simulate real exam conditions before your actual test day
